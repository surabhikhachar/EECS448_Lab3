BIG MAN DOK


BIO:
Sophomore Udoka Azubuike plays center for the Kansas Men's Basketball team.
Aside from being the absolute X-Factor for the team, Dok leads the nation in FG percentages, standing at an outstanding 76% from the field.
Dok is a vital component of the 2017-2018 KU Basketball team.
Dok is a relentless center that is always good at scoring against the defense. He plays close to the rim and close to the hearts of his fans.
With the help of Dok and the rest of the KU team, Kansas is headed for 14 straight Big 12 Conference Titles by the end of this season.
